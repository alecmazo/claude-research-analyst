📊 Financials store —
How this works (cheap by design)
  1. Browsing company history, screens, rank cards = Postgres only. No SEC, no LLM, no Railway burn.
  2. ⬇️ Pull SEC data downloads filings for the set you pick in the menu (or a custom ticker). Insert-only — never overwrites existing periods.
  3. Nightly auto (toggle below) re-checks only your companies — saved reports + watchlist — for new 10-Q/10-K periods.
  4. Monthly auto lightly refreshes the oldest names already in the store (not the whole US list). Insert-only.
  5. New filings are not live the second they drop; they land on the next nightly (followed) or monthly (store) pass, or when you pull manually.
Advanced · optional full-US gap fill

Not needed for day-to-day use. Runs one chunk of missing US-listed names (insert-only). Continuous backfill stays off unless you enable it — keeps Railway quiet.

🎯 Options Wheel — covered calls on holdings · cash-secured puts across watchlist + saved reports

Set your max assignment Δ and run a scan. Holdings are scanned for covered calls; your watchlist for cash-secured puts.

📈 Covered calls — holdings first, then your universe

▸ 📉 Cash-secured puts — across watchlist + saved reports (click to expand)
Each tenor (weekly / monthly / quarterly) shows the best strike to sell: highest annualized premium yield with assignment probability ≤ your Δ cap. Rows are ranked by the weekly annualized yield (names with only a monthly/quarterly setup trail below). Covered calls are limited to names you HOLD (you must own the shares); cash-secured puts span your whole universe — watchlist + saved reports. IV/HV > 1 (green) = implied vol above the stock's own realized vol → premium is relatively rich. Cushion = how far the stock can fall before the call trade is underwater. Δ ≈ chance of assignment. Informational only — not investment advice.
