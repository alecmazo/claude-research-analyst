# DGA Capital Management LP — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-06
**Contact:** Alec Mazo, General Partner, DGA Capital Management LP — alecmazo1@gmail.com

## Who we are
DGA Capital Management LP is a single-family office. This application is a private,
internal tool used by the General Partner to manage the family office's own and
family-managed investment accounts. It is not offered to the public.

## What data we collect
When you connect a brokerage account through SnapTrade, we receive investment
account information on a **read-only** basis: holdings, balances, account type, and a
masked (last-4) account identifier. We do **not** receive or store your
financial-institution login credentials — those are entered on the brokerage's own
page during SnapTrade's secure connection flow. We do not have the ability to place
trades or move funds.

## How we use it
Solely to display and analyze the family office's portfolio (positions,
performance, and reporting) for the GP. We do not use it for advertising, profiling,
or any purpose unrelated to managing these accounts.

## How we share it
We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own use.
Data is processed only by our service providers acting on our behalf:
- **SnapTrade** — brokerage account aggregation, to retrieve the holdings and
  balances you authorize (read-only).
- **Railway** — hosting and database (encrypted at rest; TLS in transit).

## How we protect it
Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest; the SnapTrade access credentials are
additionally encrypted at the application layer with a separately-held key. Access
is restricted to the GP and protected by multi-factor authentication on production
systems.

## Consent
We collect and process data only with consent. You provide consent through
SnapTrade's connection flow when you link an account. For family-managed accounts,
the GP holds authorization from the account owner to manage and process that
account's data.

## Retention & deletion
We retain account data only while the connection is active. You may disconnect an
account at any time, which revokes access at SnapTrade and deletes the stored data
for that connection. You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address
above.

## Your choices
You may withdraw consent and disconnect any linked account at any time, and request
access to or deletion of your data by contacting the GP.

## Changes
We will update this policy as needed and revise the "Last updated" date above.
